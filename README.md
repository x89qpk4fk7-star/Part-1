### 🏪 About the Business

Tshidi's Spaza is a small, family-run spaza shop located in Diepkloof, Soweto. The shop has been serving the community for over 10 years, providing daily essentials like bread, milk, airtime, and catering services for local events. Tshidi, the owner, wanted to modernise her business by creating an online presence that makes it easier for customers to view prices, place orders, and inquire about catering services.

### 🎯 Project Goals

1. **Increase Reach:** Help Tshidi reach more customers in Diepkloof
2. **Simplify Ordering:** Make it easy for customers to view prices and place orders
3. **Promote Catering:** Showcase catering services for community events
4. **Build Trust:** Create a professional online presence for the business
5. **Mobile-Friendly:** Ensure the site works perfectly on phones (most customers use mobile)

### 👥 Target Audience

| Audience | Needs | How We Help |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Elderly residents | Simple ordering, phone calls | Call button, clear text |
| Working parents | Quick delivery | Delivery form, WhatsApp |
| Youth | Convenience | WhatsApp button, mobile-friendly |
| Event organisers | Catering info | Catering page with inquiry form |
| Local community | Daily essentials | Price list, contact info |
